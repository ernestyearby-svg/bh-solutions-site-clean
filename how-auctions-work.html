How BH Solutions Auctions Work

BH Solutions uses a controlled dealer-only online auction format designed to move inventory quickly and transparently.

Step 1 — Dealer Registration

Dealers must submit registration and license verification before receiving auction access.

Step 2 — Dealer Approval

Once approved, dealers receive notification and access to upcoming timed auctions.

Step 3 — Online Bidding

  • Timed online bidding format
  • Dealer-only participants
  • Reserve price controlled by seller
  • Transparent bidding process

Step 4 — Payment & Pickup

  • 48-hour payment policy
  • Vehicle pickup scheduled after payment
  • All vehicles sold AS-IS unless otherwise announced
