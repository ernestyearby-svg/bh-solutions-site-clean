Dealer Requirements

BH Solutions Auto Auctions are strictly dealer-only. All bidders must provide valid dealer credentials before participating.

Required Documentation

  • Valid State Dealer License
  • Dealer License Number
  • Resale Certificate / Seller’s Permit
  • Government Photo Identification
  • Dealer Business Information
  • Primary Contact Phone & Email

Verification Process

  1. Submit dealer registration form
  2. BH Solutions verifies license information
  3. Dealer account is approved
  4. Dealer receives auction participation access
